Placements 2026

621 placement offers, ₹51 LPA highest — MLRIT closes its strongest season yet

Campus Placements Desk  ·  April 2026

This year's placement season wrapped up with 621 offers from 37 companies — numbers that would have seemed ambitious even three years ago. Cognizant led the count with 107 offers, followed by Infosys (92), HCL Tech (85), Virtusa (41), and Deloitte (20). The highest package of ₹51 LPA went to students in the CSE and CSE (AI/ML) streams.

Head of Placements Ravi Chandra P credited the outcome to a shift in how students approach the final year: "The training intensity picked up from second year. By the time companies arrived, students were already interview-ready." The institute has now placed 7,000+ students over two decades, with 400+ recruiters visiting since 2005.
