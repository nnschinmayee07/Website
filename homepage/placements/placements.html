Preparation

Training and Development

01

Aptitude and Reasoning

Structured training in quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, and data interpretation — aligned to placement test patterns of top MNCs and product companies.

02

Technical Skills

Domain-specific programmes covering DSA, Java, C++, Web Programming, MySQL, and department-specific tools — preparing students for technical interview rounds.
