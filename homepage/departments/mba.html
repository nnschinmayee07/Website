About the Department

The MBA program at MLRIT emphasizes how innovation, business and technology are inseparable for business sustenance and long term growth. It prepares students for management roles across all industries through quality education and industry exposure. The department has an active Entrepreneurship Cell and regular guest lectures from CXO-level professionals.

Vision and Mission

Vision

Centre of Excellence for Management Studies with special focus on innovation, Research, entrepreneurial skills, and team working skills.

Programme Educational Objectives

PEO 1

Apply management theories to handle business and entrepreneurial challenges

PEO 2

Develop critical thinking for better decision-making and innovation

PEO 3

Build value-based leadership competencies and contribute effectively to organizational teams

Programme Outcomes (POs)

PO1: Apply management concepts and practices to analyze business situations and make effective decisions.

PO2: Develop leadership, teamwork, and communication skills for managing diverse organizational environments.

PO3: Demonstrate ethical reasoning and social responsibility in business practices.

PO4: Utilize analytical and quantitative tools for data-driven decision making.

PO5: Exhibit entrepreneurial mindset and innovation in creating business value.

PO6: Understand global business dynamics and their implications for strategic management.
